Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start the virtual tour?

Simply click on the "Start Virtual Tour" button on the homepage or navigate to the Virtual Tour page. You can explore each floor and facility at your own pace.

Do I need to register to view the tour?

No, the basic virtual tour is free and accessible to everyone. However, registering unlocks premium features like live Q&A sessions and personalized guides.

How many floors does the school have?

Our campus features 5 floors with over 120 rooms including classrooms, laboratories, gymnasium, library, cafeteria, and specialized facilities.

Can I schedule an in-person visit?

Yes! You can schedule an in-person campus tour by contacting us through the Contact page or calling our admissions office at +63 (02) 8123-4567 or +63 917-123-4567.

What facilities are available in the school?

We offer state-of-the-art facilities including a modern gymnasium, science laboratories (Physics, Chemistry, Biology), computer labs with 60+ workstations, a library with 50,000+ books, art and music studios, an auditorium, and a cafeteria.

Is the virtual tour available 24/7?

Yes! The virtual tour is available anytime, anywhere. You can explore our campus at your convenience.

What is the difference between Basic and Premium tours?

The Basic tour (free) includes virtual tour access, campus map, and photo gallery. The Premium tour (₱950) adds live Q&A sessions with staff, personalized tour guides, and priority booking for in-person visits.

How can I contact the school?

You can reach us through the Contact page, email us at info@schooltour.edu.ph, or call +63 (02) 8123-4567 or +63 917-123-4567. Our office hours are Monday-Friday, 8AM-5PM.
