## 🎬 What Video Should You Download?

### **Recommended Video Type:**
**"School Campus Virtual Tour" or "University Campus Walkthrough"**

### **Video Specs:**
- **Duration:** 2-5 minutes
- **Resolution:** 1080p (1920x1080)
- **Format:** MP4 (most compatible)
- **Content:** Walking through hallways, classrooms, facilities
- **Style:** Professional, smooth camera movement

---

## 📥 Where to Download FREE School Tour Videos:

### **Option 1: Pexels (BEST - 100% Free)**
1. Go to: https://www.pexels.com/videos/
2. Search: "school tour" or "campus walkthrough" or "university building"
3. Download any video you like (100% free, no attribution needed)
4. **Recommended videos:**
   - Search "school hallway"
   - Search "classroom empty"
   - Search "university campus"

### **Option 2: Pixabay (Free)**
1. Go to: https://pixabay.com/videos/
2. Search: "school" or "education building"
3. Download (free, no account needed)

### **Option 3: Videvo (Free)**
1. Go to: https://www.videvo.net
2. Search: "school" or "campus"
3. Filter by "Free" license

### **Option 4: Make Your Own!**
- Use your phone camera
- Walk through your actual school
- 2-3 minutes is perfect
- Keep camera steady

## 📁 How to Add Your Video:

### **Step 1: Create folders**
In your project folder, create:
```
GSYSTEMNISIRDONGKOL/
├── videos/           ← Create this folder
│   └── school-tour.mp4  ← Put your video here
├── images/           ← Create this folder  
│   └── video-poster.jpg ← Optional thumbnail
```

### **Step 2: Rename your video**
- Download your video
- Rename it to: **`school-tour.mp4`**
- Put it in the `videos` folder

### **Step 3: Optional - Add thumbnail**
- Take a screenshot from your video
- Save it as: **`video-poster.jpg`**
- Put it in the `images` folder
- This shows before video plays

### **Step 4: Test locally**
- Open `tour.html` in your browser
- Video should play!
